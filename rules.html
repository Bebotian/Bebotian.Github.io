📋 Rules

Read these carefully before submitting a record.

Failure to follow these rules will result in your record being rejected or your account being banned.

🎥 Video Requirements
1.
Full completion required. Your video must show the entire level from 0% to 100% with no cuts or skips.
2.
YouTube only. Only YouTube links are accepted. Unlisted videos are allowed, but private videos will be rejected.
3.
Raw footage preferred. Your video must show the in-game attempt percentage or clicks. Livestream VODs are accepted if they include these.
4.
FPS must be visible. A visible FPS counter or consistent frame rate is required to verify legitimacy.
🎮 Gameplay Requirements
5.
No hacks or cheats. Use of any hacks, including but not limited to speedhacks, noclip, or auto-clickers will result in a permanent ban.
6.
Physics must be standard. Low physics (60Hz) or high physics (240Hz+) records may require additional review.
7.
Official level only. The level you completed must match the current version on the list. Records on outdated or modified versions will be rejected.
📝 Submission Requirements
8.
One submission per level. Do not submit the same level multiple times. Duplicate submissions will be removed.
9.
Correct level name. The level name in your submission must exactly match the name on the list.
10.
Be patient. Records can take several days to review. Do not resubmit or contact admins to speed up the process.
