ℹ️ About

Everything you need to know about this list.

⚔ What is the Demon List?

The Demon List is a community-maintained ranking of the hardest rated levels in Geometry Dash. Levels are ranked by difficulty as determined by our team of reviewers.

Players can submit verified completions to be featured on the list and appear on the stats leaderboard.

📋 How does it work?

Levels are added, removed, and repositioned by our admin team based on community consensus. Every placement is carefully discussed before being made official.

To get your completion listed, create an account, submit your YouTube video through the Submit Record page, and wait for a reviewer to verify it.
